Mr. Vijay Palaskar

Secretary
Badmintion Sports Association Buldhana

Dear Players/Parents/public/Officials,

I distinctly understand that my pre-eminent stature and passion for the game has conferred me with the honor of being the president of BSAB. I thank everyone who has reposed faith in my abilities and I am obliged to return the favour by serving Buldana Badminton to my full capacity. I have spent enough effort to comprehend the rich tradition and the pathway of BSAB through its good and bad times. Be that as it may, I am not so glad for the fact that we haven't had enough representations at National Level either at play or at administration. Having seen the talent aplenty in our district, state, I can't help thinking there is not enough motivation and guidance to groom players. I have therefore instructed the committee and the officials to be as fleet footed as the players and work overtime. I am delighted, in no time, they have started to deliver.

On a happy note, BSAB has hosted One State Ranking Tournaments, the prestigious Inter District Championships, implemented Tournament Software for a fair, transparent and structured manner to conduct tournaments, established a new Ranking System to favor points to a larger pool of players, conducted state camps before all championships and Inter - District competitions; all in just the last few months of 2022 and I hope it served as an antidote to the previous delays.

Vision:

To create and develop committed, knowledgeable, qualified and excellent world class badminton coaches & Players

Mission:

To regulate, promote, manage and develop the sport of 'Badminton' in the district transparently.

To create comprehensively established progressive pathway for Badminton Players, Coaches and other technical persons of the district.".
